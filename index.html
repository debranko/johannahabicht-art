About the Artist

Johanna Habicht creates abstract expressionist works that explore the interplay between color, movement, and raw emotion. Each canvas is a conversation between intention and spontaneity, where fluid acrylics meet gold leaf accents to create pieces that shimmer with life.

Working primarily in acrylic with gold leaf embellishments, the paintings embrace bold contrasts and organic forms, inviting the viewer to find their own meaning within each composition.
